Description

This is vegan grilled cheese, not only is it tasty, but meatless.

This grilled cheese will take about 30 minutes to make, below will be the ingredients.

ingredients

  • Vegan Cheese slices(Daiya is a highly popular brand)
  • Hawaian bread slices
  • Butter
  • Pan
  • Honey
  • Grilled Cheese maker

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat.Add the butter and let it spread out in the pot
  2. Get two slices of your Hawaian bread
  3. Two slices of cheese
  4. Put a table spoon of honey/syrup in the pan and spin it around
  5. Put the two cheese slices on top of the bread and put the other slice of bread on top to make a sandwich
  6. Put it in the Grill cheese machine
    7. <1i>Wait for it to finish it should take about 5 or less minutes
  7. Than simmmer each side of bread in pan sauce for a about 1 minutes and your done>
